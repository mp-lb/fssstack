# Alternatives and Competitors

## Named by the user

- **Spreadsheets** — PMs tag and bucket exported tickets by hand.
- **Productboard** — feedback-to-roadmap SaaS; ingests customer input but is built around manual tagging and feature voting.
- **Dovetail** — qualitative research repository; strong at tagging and synthesis across interviews and notes, weaker at ticket-stream automation.
- **Manually reading tickets** — PM or support lead skims Zendesk directly and forms an impression.

## Replacement behaviors

- Doing nothing; relying on the loudest customer or the most recent escalation.
- Asking support leads for a monthly "top issues" summary.
- Ad hoc Slack threads or Notion docs where PMs paste interesting tickets.

## How each handles the core job

| Alternative | Pulls Zendesk automatically | Clusters recurring issues | Links themes to accounts | Outputs roadmap-ready notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Spreadsheets | No | Manual | Manual | Manual |
| Productboard | Via integration, manual tagging emphasis | Partial, tag-driven | Yes for linked customers | Yes, inside its own roadmap |
| Dovetail | Not natively for tickets | Yes for research data | Limited | Research-style, not roadmap-prioritized |
| Manual reading | No | No | No | No |
