# Market Context

## Chosen frame

**Support-ticket-to-roadmap intelligence for B2B SaaS product managers.**

This frame keeps the product on its strongest ground: turning an existing support data stream into account-aware, roadmap-ready themes.

## Why this frame, not the obvious ones

- **Not "feedback management" (Productboard's frame):** competing here forces TestApp into feature-vote and roadmap-tool comparisons it does not need to win, and obscures the Zendesk-native automation advantage.
- **Not "user research platform" (Dovetail's frame):** that frame implies interviews, notes, and qualitative tagging — work the product does not do.
- **Not "support analytics":** support-analytics tools answer questions for support leaders (resolution time, CSAT). TestApp's buyer is the PM, and the output is roadmap input.

## Why this frame helps the ideal customer recognize the fit

- Names the data source they already have (Zendesk tickets).
- Names the artifact they already need (roadmap input).
- Skips categories that would force them to evaluate TestApp against tools twice its scope.
