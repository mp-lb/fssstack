# Unique Capabilities

Each capability is tested against named alternatives: spreadsheets, Productboard, Dovetail, and manual reading.

## 1. Automatic clustering of the full Zendesk ticket stream into themes

- Spreadsheets and manual reading require a human to read and bucket.
- Dovetail clusters research data, not the live support-ticket stream.
- Productboard accepts ticket inputs but leans on manual tagging rather than automatic recurring-issue clustering.

## 2. Account-weighted theme view, not just ticket-count view

- Themes are linked to the specific B2B accounts they affect.
- Spreadsheets and manual reading can do this only with sustained human effort.
- Productboard supports linked customers per feature request but is not built around account-impact rollups across clustered ticket themes.
- Dovetail is research-centric and does not natively model B2B account impact on support volume.

## 3. Roadmap-ready prioritized output as the primary artifact

- The product's output is a prioritized export designed to feed an existing roadmap process.
- Productboard expects you to live inside its own roadmap; it does not produce a portable prioritized note.
- Dovetail produces research insights, not roadmap-prioritized notes.
- Spreadsheets and manual reading produce whatever the human produces.

## Rejected as not unique

- "Easy to use," "AI-powered," "saves time," "modern UI" — any alternative could claim these.
- "Integrates with Zendesk" — Productboard also integrates with Zendesk; integration alone is not the differentiator.
