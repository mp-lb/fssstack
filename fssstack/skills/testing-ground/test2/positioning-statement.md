# Positioning Statement

## Statement

For product managers at small B2B SaaS teams,
who today rely on spreadsheets, manual ticket reading, or heavier feedback suites like Productboard and Dovetail,
TestApp is a support-ticket-to-roadmap intelligence tool
that turns the Zendesk ticket stream into account-weighted, prioritized roadmap notes,
unlike spreadsheets and manual reading, which do not scale, and unlike Productboard or Dovetail, which require manual tagging or are built for research rather than live ticket streams,
because TestApp automatically clusters recurring issues from Zendesk, links each theme to the accounts it affects, and exports the result as roadmap-ready prioritized notes.

## Stress test

- **Specific:** names the user (B2B SaaS PM), the data (Zendesk), and the artifact (roadmap notes).
- **Differentiated:** explicitly contrasts with each named alternative.
- **Understandable:** the frame is a familiar workflow (tickets → roadmap), not a new category invention.
- **Grounded in evidence:** each clause maps to a capability listed in `product-features.md`.
- **Attractive to the ideal customer:** addresses the exact bandwidth and account-impact problems described in `ideal-customer.md`.
- **Hard to copy without lying:** spreadsheets and manual reading cannot claim automatic clustering; Productboard cannot claim to be lighter-weight; Dovetail cannot claim live ticket-stream prioritization.
