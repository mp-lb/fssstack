# Open Questions

## Product reality

- Does clustering run on the full live ticket stream or on imported batches?
- How are themes prioritized — by ticket count, by account count, by account revenue, or by a configurable weight?
- What does "roadmap notes" output look like? Markdown, CSV, a Linear/Jira push, an email digest?
- Are there integrations beyond Zendesk (Intercom, Front, HubSpot Service)? If not, is Zendesk-only an intentional positioning constraint?
- How is account identity attached to a ticket — Zendesk organization field, custom field, or external CRM lookup?

## Customer reality

- What ticket volume is the lower bound where TestApp becomes worth adopting?
- Is the buyer the PM, the head of product, or the founder? (Affects pricing and sales motion.)
- Are existing users replacing spreadsheets, replacing Productboard, or adopting alongside Productboard?

## Evidence we do not yet have

- Landing page, pricing, screenshots, product docs.
- Customer testimonials or quotes that confirm the value drivers in the buyer's own words.
- Sales-call language about what alternative the customer was previously using.

## Positioning risks to validate

- Is "support-ticket-to-roadmap intelligence" a frame customers already use, or does it need to be taught?
- Does Productboard's Zendesk integration close enough of the gap that the differentiator becomes "lighter and automatic" rather than "uniquely capable"?
- Is the small-B2B-SaaS-PM segment large enough to anchor positioning, or is it a beachhead toward a broader segment?
